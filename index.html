Sleep Story

Luna and the Sleepy Moon

Luna the little puppy curled up inside her soft moon nest.

The sky was quiet, the stars were glowing, and the clouds floated gently around her.

“It is time to rest,” whispered the sleepy moon.

Luna took a slow breath in… and a soft breath out.

Her blanket felt warm. Her paws felt cosy. Her eyes began to feel heavy.

The moon smiled softly and whispered, “You are safe. You are loved. You did a wonderful job today.”

Luna snuggled deeper into her moon nest and drifted into a peaceful dream.
