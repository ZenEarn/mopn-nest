Parent Info

About Moon Nest

Moon Nest is a calming bedtime routine app designed to help children slow down, feel safe, and prepare for sleep.

Luna is the sleepy puppy who guides children through bedtime with a routine checklist, calming breathing, gentle sounds, sleep timer, and a relaxing sleep story.

Moon Nest is designed as a quiet bedtime helper. It does not need child accounts, messages, location, or personal details.

For best results, use Moon Nest with low screen brightness, gentle volume, and a calm bedtime routine.

Privacy & Terms

Privacy Policy

Moon Nest is designed as a calm bedtime helper for children and families. The app does not require child accounts, names, emails, messages, location, photos, or personal details.

Moon Nest stores bedtime stars only on the device using local storage. This helps the app remember progress without sending information to us.

The app uses audio, video, and images only to provide bedtime routines, calming sounds, and Luna’s sleep story.

Terms of Use

Moon Nest is a general bedtime routine and relaxation app. It is not medical advice, sleep therapy, or a health service.

Parents and carers are responsible for deciding whether Moon Nest is suitable for their child.
