About Moon Nest

Moon Nest is a calming bedtime routine app designed to help children slow down, feel safe, and prepare for sleep.

Luna is the sleepy puppy who guides children through bedtime with a routine checklist, calming breathing, gentle sounds, and a relaxing sleep story.

This app is designed as a quiet bedtime helper. It does not need child accounts, messages, location, or personal details.

For best results, use Moon Nest as part of a calm bedtime routine with low screen brightness and gentle volume.
