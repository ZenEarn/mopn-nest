Parent Info

About Moon Nest

Moon Nest is a calming bedtime routine app designed to help children slow down, feel safe, and prepare for sleep.

Luna, Fable and Twinkle guide children through a routine checklist, calming breathing, gentle sounds, bedtime stories and sleep rewards.

Moon Nest does not need child accounts, messages, location or personal details.

Privacy & Terms

Privacy Policy

Moon Nest is designed as a calm bedtime helper for children and families. The app does not require child accounts, names, emails, messages, location, photos or personal details.

Bedtime stars are stored only on the device using local storage.

Terms of Use

Moon Nest is a general bedtime routine and relaxation app. It is not medical advice, sleep therapy or a health service.

Parents and carers are responsible for deciding whether Moon Nest is suitable for their child.
